Billing Information

Upon check-out, Hotel Taj provides a comprehensive bill that includes the charges for both the room and food. This consolidated bill reflects the total amount due for your stay, making it convenient for you to settle the payment in one transaction.

Additionally, if you require separate bills for room charges and food expenses, our staff is happy to accommodate your request. Simply inform our front desk or the food services team, and they will ensure that you receive separate bills detailing the charges for each category.
